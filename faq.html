Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to the most common questions from governments, operators, partners, and investors.

Mahasarathi is not a ride-hailing service. We do not own vehicles, set fares, or control drivers. Instead, we act as a neutral mobility layer that integrates public transport (buses, metro, rail), semi-public transport (shared autos), and on-demand services.

Think of Mahasarathi as the UPI of mobility — interoperable, neutral, and designed to work with everyone.

Governments and RTCs gain a unified, real-time view of their transport systems without losing control. Mahasarathi helps improve service reliability, reduce losses, and plan capacity using data — without locking authorities into a single vendor.

Mahasarathi is both — and neither exclusively. At its core, it is an integration platform. Governments, operators, and private apps can all plug into the same interoperable layer, while commuters benefit from a unified experience.

  • Optional premium features for consumers
